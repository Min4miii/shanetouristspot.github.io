ABOUT

Oksi Tour

We are a team of passionate travelers who love to explore the hidden gems of the Philippines. We created this website to share our love of Occidental Mindoro with the world. We believe that Occidental Mindoro is one of the most beautiful and underrated provinces in the Philippines. It is home to a variety of tourist attractions, including stunning beaches, lush mountains, cascading waterfalls, and vibrant coral reefs. We are committed to providing our visitors with the information they need to plan their perfect trip to Occidental Mindoro. Our website includes detailed information on all of the different tourist attractions in the province, as well as tips on how to get there, where to stay, and what to do.

TOURISTS SPOT

Occidental Mindoro is a province in the Philippines known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse culture. It is home to a variety of tourist attractions, including pristine beaches, lush mountains, cascading waterfalls, and vibrant coral reefs.

Apo Reef Natural Park

Apo Reef is the second largest coral reef system in the world and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling, and is home to a variety of marine life, including colorful fish, sea turtles, and dolphins.

Lubang Island

Lubang Island is an island with white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush rainforests. It is a popular destination for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts.

Mount Iglit-Baco

Mount iglit-Baco is home to the endangered tamaraw, a water buffalo that is native in the Philippines. The Park also offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Grace Island Resort

The resort is known for its stunning natural beauty, with white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush green mountains. It is also home to a variety of marine life, including colorful fish, sea turtles, and dolphins.

Sablayan Mangyan Village

The Sablayan Mangyan Village is a place where you can learn about the indigenous Mangyan culture. The Mangyan are the original inhabitants of Occidental Mindoro and have a rich culture that is unique to the province.

Sablayan Zipline

The Sablayan Zipline Adventure is the longest island-to-island zipline in the world, spanning 1.7 kilometers from Presing Park on the mainland of Occidental Mindoro to Pandan Island.
